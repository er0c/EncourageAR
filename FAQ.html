FAQ

Q - Do I have to expose my real identity?

A - No, we give our users the option to be annonymous and we allow our users to to share what they want about themselves. One of our values being safety, we make sure that our users are protected throughout their expereince with our app.



Q - How do I get started?

A - Getting started is fairly easy! You would sign up with your email, username, and password to create an account.



Q - How is my privacy protected?

A - Our user's account would be automatically private, meaning their accounts can not be viewed by anyone unless they are on the user's friendlist or the user's account is switched to public.



Q - How can I be matched with a counselor?

A - Being matched with a counselor is not difficult. We would need personal health information and we would manually pick a counselor for you to be matched with. DUE TO HIPPA LAWS and NHC LAWS your information would be stored and protected by a high encryption that is difficult to crack/infiltrate.



Q - What is the verification process?

A - YOUR VERIFICATION: Once all of your information is sent in we would verify the all of the documents with your insurance company along with double checking the validity of the documents.
COUNSELOR VERFICATION: Counselors are required to interview in person, present their resume, and many other documents so we can verify if they are the right fit to be apart of Encourage. We would also check the counselor's background, checking criminal records, and past occupations.



Q - Is Enourage right for me?

A - Encourage has everything for everyone! We have servers running 24/7 allowing many to communcaite regulary through text and call. We also have AR servers where many can go an expereince dance classes, cooking classes, and even experience concerts in the comfort of their own home.



Q - How does direct messages work?

A - Direct messages is very easy to manage. Either you can directly text to them or in AR you can tap on their profile and request to speak to them privately.



Q - How does phone calls work?

A - Phone calls would work normally however if you implement AR into your calls you able to see them in your own home.



Q - How does live video calls work?

A - Live video calls would work by scanning what you present your camera. Allowing the other person to project an augmented reality hologram of you in their home.



Q - How do I use augmented reality(AR) during a call?

A - There would be an option in your settings to toggle on AR and there would be an option to turn it on during a call. Once AR is turned on the person you are calling can see a hologram of what your camera can see.



Q - How much does it cost?

A - There isn't a determined cost yet however, we want everyone to be able to access Envourage. Meaning we will cut costs or have financial plans to lower the costs.



Q - What are the free features?

A - The free features are being able to use ar in calls, video calls, and the 24 hour servers that we provide so you can socialize with people around the world. However, Therapy will have a low fee for users. (we really want therapy to be free however, we currently do not have a way to make money and supply our project.)



Q - Will everything be private?

A - Yes! Everything will be private when you first make your account. Only your username would be shown to others on servers. However, if you turn public on, your account would certain things like your bio, intrest, and when you join Encourage.
