Why Us?

Our overall goal for Encourage is to provide a safe space for those who don't have a place to express themselves freely. We want to give everyone who is struggling from quarantine a way to communicate in a more personal and engaging way through augmented reality and increase mental health awareness.

Who?

We created Encourage for people who are struggling from quarantine and isolation. Encourage will allow them to interact and socialize safely with other people in the comfort of their homes.

What?

We help people by assiting them with mental health issues and giving them a place to socialize more and be more personal over the internet using AR.

How?

We plan on creating servers that are up 24/7. Where many of our users can join at any time to socialize about specifics interest that are dedicated. We also have private servers where users can go and get matched with a counselor. The counselor would listien and give them advice.(like a therapist)

Why?

Our intentions for Encourage is to create a change that society won't see mental health as a small problem but a main problem that is constantly being dealt with. We want to increase the awareness of mental health and at the same time help thoose who are suffering from mental health.

Our Core Values

Every person on our team upholds and maintains our core values : Dependability, Safety, Comfort, Love, and Unity.
